Detailed Description

The layer 4 RF experimental protocol.

These functions create the experimental RF layer 4 (transport) protocol. The protocol is a fixed length protocol and provides no error detection. Packet fragmentation is not supported and therefor the layer 4 protocol packets much be equal to or smaller than the maximum transmissible unit of the lower layers.
